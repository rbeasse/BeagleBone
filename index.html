Welcome

by Ryan Beasse

Hello world, this is a website for controlling and monitoring my BeagleBone Black. Everything you see is coded by myself, using the power of NodeJS with Bonescript and socket.io. Enjoy your visit, however remember that most of the functionality requires you to see the board itself.
